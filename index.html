Manifesto / 01

Our mission

Secrets belong to people,
not platforms.

Digital ownership should not depend on a company keeping your account alive. Meta Secret builds open-source cryptographic systems where authority begins on devices you control and access is shared only by deliberate consent.

Storage can be replaced. Infrastructure can disappear. Your ability to prove who you are—and recover what is yours—should survive both.

Protocol / 02

Design constraints

We remove single
points of authority.

01

Device controlled

Authority lives with approved devices and cryptographic identities—not a central account database.

02

Ciphertext outside

Secrets are encrypted before leaving the client. Storage providers carry data without owning access.

03

Consent required

New participants and sensitive operations require visible, deliberate authorization.

04

Open machinery

Security deserves inspection. The code, protocols, and trade-offs are built in the open.
